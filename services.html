    • Instrumentation

      Our services for field instrumentation include the installing and monitoring devices, which obtain data for the analysis of long term behavior of soil movement, ground water table, soil-structure interaction and structure deformations. In the case of soil movement, ground water table and soil-structure interaction, GEOCONSULT can design of the instrumentation system, install and monitor, process data and provide a final analysis of the results. For structures, we can install and monitor instrumentation and submit data to the client for further processing and analysis. Our experience in instrumentation services includes the installation of piezometers, inclinometers, strain gages, rebar strainmeters, earth pressure cells, extensometers, tiltmeters, temperature and humidity probes and accelerometers. We have designed data collection systems for automatic reading and storage.
